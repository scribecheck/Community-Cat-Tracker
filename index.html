About the Cat Tracker

You can browse profiles of community cats in the neighborhood! All the information and images are shared by neighbors in Sendabori, Matsudo City.
If you see a cat in the neighborhood, go to the cat's profile and click the "I saw you!" button.
The "I saw you today!" button does not save personal information such as your name, address, or location. It only counts how many times it is clicked per day.
